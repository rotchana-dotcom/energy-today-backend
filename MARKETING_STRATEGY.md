## Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Closed Testing (Current)
**Duration:** 2-4 weeks  
**Goal:** Validate product-market fit, fix bugs, gather testimonials

**Activities:**
- Recruit 50-100 testers per country (200-400 total)
- Collect feedback via form/email
- Fix critical bugs
- Gather success stories for marketing
- Test subscription conversion rate

**Success Metrics:**
- 80%+ testers complete onboarding
- 30%+ try Pro features (with promo code)
- <5 critical bugs reported
- 4+ star average rating from testers
- At least 10 testimonials collected

### Phase 2: Soft Launch (Production)
**Duration:** 2-4 weeks  
**Goal:** Launch to production, limited marketing, monitor metrics

**Activities:**
- Promote from Closed Testing to Production
- Soft launch (no major marketing push)
- Monitor crash rates, reviews, conversions
- A/B test pricing (if needed)
- Refine onboarding based on data

**Success Metrics:**
- <1% crash rate
- 4+ star rating on Play Store
- 5-10% free-to-Pro conversion rate
- <5% refund rate
- Positive user reviews

### Phase 3: Full Launch (Marketing Push)
**Duration:** Ongoing  
**Goal:** Scale user acquisition, optimize conversion

**Activities:**
- Launch marketing campaigns
- Content marketing (blog, social media)
- Paid ads (Google, Facebook, LinkedIn)
- PR and media outreach
- Influencer partnerships
- Referral program

**Success Metrics:**
- 1,000+ downloads/month
- 10%+ free-to-Pro conversion
- $5,000+ MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- 4.5+ star rating maintained
- <3% churn rate

## Conversion Funnel Optimization

### Current Funnel
1. **Download** (100%)
2. **Onboarding Complete** (target: 80%)
3. **Active User (7 days)** (target: 50%)
4. **Pro Trial Started** (target: 20%)
5. **Pro Subscriber** (target: 10%)

### Optimization Tactics

**Improve Onboarding (Step 2):**
- Clear value proposition on first screen
- Quick setup (< 2 minutes)
- Show immediate value (today's score)
- Gamification (progress indicators)

**Increase Engagement (Step 3):**
- Daily notifications with energy score
- Quick actions widget
- Outcome logging reminders
- Success streak tracking

**Drive Trial Starts (Step 4):**
- Show locked Pro features prominently
- "Upgrade to see why" CTAs
- Limited-time trial offers
- Social proof (testimonials)

**Convert to Paid (Step 5):**
- Clear ROI messaging
- Before/after success stories
- Money-back guarantee
- Easy cancellation (builds trust)

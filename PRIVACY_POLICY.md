# Privacy Policy for Energy Today

**Last updated:** December 28, 2024

## Introduction

Energy Today ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application Energy Today (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

When you use Energy Today, you may provide the following information:

- **Date of Birth** - Used to calculate your personal energy patterns and timing recommendations
- **Name** (optional) - Used for personalization within the app
- **Journal Entries** - Your personal notes, reflections, and mood tracking data
- **Voice Notes** (optional) - Audio recordings you create within journal entries

### Automatically Collected Information

The App may automatically collect:

- **App Usage Data** - Which features you use and how often (stored locally)
- **Energy Calculations** - Your daily energy scores and alignment data (stored locally)

## How We Store Your Information

**All your data is stored locally on your device.** Energy Today does not:

- Upload your data to our servers
- Share your data with third parties
- Sync your data to the cloud
- Transmit your personal information over the internet

Your journal entries, voice notes, date of birth, and all calculated energy data remain private on your device.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

- Calculate daily energy scores based on your birth date
- Generate personalized timing recommendations
- Display energy forecasts and optimal activity suggestions
- Store your journal entries and reflections for your personal review
- Provide insights about your personal energy patterns

All processing happens locally on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

## Data Sharing and Third Parties

**We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal information to any third parties.**

Energy Today does not integrate with:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Cloud storage providers

Your data stays entirely on your device.

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information:

- All data is stored in encrypted local storage on your device
- No data transmission over networks
- No cloud backups (unless you manually enable device backups through your OS)
- Access to the app is controlled by your device's security (PIN, fingerprint, Face ID)

## Your Data Rights

### Access Your Data

## Children's Privacy

Energy Today is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

## In-App Purchases

Energy Today offers optional Pro features through in-app purchases. Payment information is processed by Google Play Store or Apple App Store and is not collected or stored by us.

## Permissions

Energy Today may request the following device permissions:

- **Notifications** - To send you reminders during high-energy windows (optional)
- **Microphone** - To record voice notes in journal entries (optional, only when you choose to record)
- **Storage** - To save and read your local data

All permissions are optional and can be managed in your device settings.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
- Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
- Updating the "Last updated" date
- Notifying you within the app (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained on your device until you:
- Manually delete it through Settings
- Uninstall the app
- Clear app data through your device settings

We do not retain any data on our servers because we do not collect it.

## International Users

Energy Today is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no international data transfers occur.
